About

Hi, my name is Manou (short for Manouchehr). I’m a junior at Stanford majoring in Symbolic Systems (HCI track) and minoring in Creative Writing.

My lifelong interest in storytelling has led me across multiple mediums (prose, film, and now interactive computer experiences) each informing how I approach the others.

I’m interested in an antidisciplinary approach to storytelling: conveying narrative by whatever means a story demands, and exploring how technology and form allow us to push boundaries and invent new kinds of stories.
